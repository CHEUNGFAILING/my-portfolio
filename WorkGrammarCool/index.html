Grammar Cool
- Japanese Grammar Learning App

Results

When using the brand new Grammar Cool to learn Japanese grammar, users will no longer feel fatigued or uninterested.

The interface has been transformed from black and white to a more dynamic and engaging design. Example sentences are now presented in a clear and organized structure, and Japanese text and translations are separated for better comprehension. Besides, users have the option to toggle the display of furigana which provides the readings for the kanji characters.

These improvements not only prevent monotony but also enhance learning efficiency, enabling users to quickly locate and concentrate on their desired learning points.

Overview

This is a Japanese grammar learning app that provides basic explanations and example sentences for each grammar point. It helps learners gain a better understanding of the grammar concepts.

Challenge

Monotonous Page Design
The overall page design has a single color scheme with only subtle horizontal lines separating each section. When there is a large amount of text, users may find it visually taxing and may lack the patience to delve into the learning points.

Congested Layout
The absence of a clear distinction between example sentences and their translations, as well as the lack of paragraph breaks in the explanations, creates difficulties for users in navigating and comprehending the material. This can discourage users from carefully examining the example sentences, leading to challenges in their learning process.

Solution

Clear Section Differentiation
Using colors to differentiate each section allows users to easily follow the content.

Segmented Paragraphs
Each example sentence is presented individually, along with its corresponding translation. This clear separation not only enhances the reading experience for learners but also allows them to efficiently locate and focus on the specific learning points they are interested in.


Adding Furigana Option
In the upper-right corner of each section, users are provided with an eye icon that can be used to enable or disable the display of furigana. This convenient feature eliminates the need for users to search for the pronunciation of kanji characters separately, streamlining their learning process.

Design

The brand new Grammar Cool offers users a delightful experience with its clear and organized page layout. Users can easily revisit specific points without the need for additional searching, making their learning journey seamless and efficient.

This user-centric design of Grammar Cool promotes efficiency and enhances user satisfaction, making it a valuable tool for language learners.

Idea

Color Scheme
Originally, I had planned to assign different colors to each label, but I realized that it would only make the page look cluttered and less concise. Moreover, not every grammar point has multiple learning sections, which could result in inconsistency.

The primary purpose of adding colors is to enhance visual distinction and aesthetics, rather than simply adding colors for the sake of it. Therefore, I opted for lighter shades of blue, pink, and gray, which give the page a more elegant and spacious appearance.

Furigana Display
Considering the diverse range of grammar proficiency levels, it is vital to cater to the needs of beginners in Grammar Cool.

Adding furigana is advantageous as it help reduce learning obstacles and aid in expanding vocabulary.

Gains

Improving Grammar Cool posed challenges for me in terms of color selection and ensuring that each label corresponded to its respective section.

However, these challenges expanded my thinking and through continuous experimentation, I was able to find the optimal solution.
