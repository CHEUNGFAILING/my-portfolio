Carrier Oil
- Media Website

Results

Simple and straightforward guidance enables users to quickly find the information and content they are looking for. Vibrant visuals and adaptable buttons captivate users' attention. Besides, users can stay updated by utilizing the subscription system.

Users can progressively explore the desired contextual information without being overwhelmed by presenting all the information at once.

Overview

This website offers knowledge and articles about Carrier Oil, mainly featuring a Home Page, Service Page(page Oil), Blog Page, and About Page.

Design

Home Page

Landing Page
The landing page highlights the user's specific skincare concerns, and the "See More" button directs them to the Oil Page, where they can explore the range of carrier oils available to address their skin issues.

Guiding
Carrier oils are classified according to skin types, enabling users to easily identify the most suitable carrier oil for their individual needs.

These flip-able labels add to the aesthetic appeal of the page.

Bottom - Staying Connected
The email subscription feature at the bottom facilitates ongoing communication with users, thereby increasing website engagement and page views.

Home Page - Guiding Section

Introduction
Upon clicking a label on the Home Page, for example, "Dry Skin," users will be taken to a dedicated page that provides information about dry skin and recommends suitable carrier oils for dry skin care.

Items
The listed carrier oils are specifically recommended for dry skin, and users can click the "Details" button to access more comprehensive information related to each oil.

Dynamic Pictures
The pictures come to life with dynamic effects when the mouse hovers over them. By clicking the "See more" button, users will be directed to the Oil Page, where all the carrier oils are displayed.

Service Page

Item List
This page is designed to list items one by one, providing a combination of pictures and text descriptions for each item.

Visually, the curved dashed lines and circular icons resemble fruits hanging from stretching tree branches.

Dynamic Pictures
As the mouse hovers over the item, it comes to life, making the webpage visually captivating and effectively capturing the user's attention.

"Next" Button
All dashed circle icons with text are functional buttons. Users can navigate to the next page and explore other items by clicking the "Next" button.

Service Page - Item Details

Forward Path
Clicking either the "Details" button or the name label of the item will redirect users to the Item Details Page, where they can find more in-depth information about the specific carrier oil, including its properties, ingredients and effects, and any additional relevant details.

Return Path
The "Back" button will navigate users to the previous page they were on, while the "See more" button will redirect users to the Oil Page.

Blog Page

Article Updates
This section defaults to sorting articles by the latest order, and users can also switch to viewing articles sorted by popularity by clicking the "Hot" button. At the same time, the search function allows users to find specific content by article title.

Categories
In this section, users can select labels to find articles that meet their specific requirements.

Bottom - Staying Connected
If users are interested in the articles on this website and don't want to miss any updates, they can subscribe directly at the bottom for regular newsletters.

About Page

This is the About Page, which contains the mission statement and contact information for this website.

In my personal opinion, the About Page should be concise and still maintain the style of the website.

Gains

I have to admit that I still have some shortcomings in web design. However, while working on Carrier Oil, I have learned a lot, such as compatibility and animation.

Although there are areas where I fall short, it also signifies the significant growth potential I have. Therefore, I will dedicate myself to observing and understanding user behavior better to enhance my skills.
